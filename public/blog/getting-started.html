Getting Started with ChatClaw

From install to first conversation in under 5 minutes.

Step 1: Download & Install

ChatClaw is available on three platforms:

Platform How to Get It
iOS App Store or TestFlight (beta)
Android Google Play or Firebase App Distribution (beta)
Web Open chatclaw.app in any modern browser

The ChatClaw app is lightweight ‚Äî under 50MB on mobile. No heavy downloads, no waiting.

Step 2: Create Your Account

Open ChatClaw and sign up:

  1. Tap Get Started
  2. Choose your sign-up method ‚Äî Email, Google, or Apple
  3. Verify your email (if using email sign-up)
  4. You're in

Firebase Authentication handles your account securely. Your credentials are encrypted and never stored in plain text.

Step 3: First Voice Interaction

This is where ChatClaw comes alive.

  1. Tap the microphone button at the bottom of the screen
  2. Speak naturally ‚Äî try something like:
    • "What can you do?"
    • "Tell me about the weather today"
    • "Create a to-do list for my morning routine"
  3. Watch the AI generate a response with an adaptive interface ‚Äî not just text, but cards, lists, or interactive elements tailored to your request

Tips for best results:

  • Speak clearly at a normal pace
  • Be specific about what you want
  • You can ask follow-up questions ‚Äî ChatClaw maintains context

Step 4: Switch Themes

ChatClaw ships with the Factory Floor design system in two modes.

  1. Go to Settings (gear icon, top right)
  2. Tap Appearance
  3. Choose Dark or Light theme

The dark theme is the signature look ‚Äî industrial, clean, monospace. The light theme offers the same aesthetic in a brighter palette. Both are designed for extended use without eye strain.

Troubleshooting

Microphone not working? Check your device permissions: Settings ‚Üí ChatClaw ‚Üí Microphone ‚Üí Enable

Voice not recognized? Ensure you're in a reasonably quiet environment. ChatClaw works well with background noise, but extreme conditions may affect accuracy.

Can't connect to OpenClaw? Verify your gateway URL is correct and the OpenClaw service is running. Check your network connection.
