ChatClaw FAQ: Everything You Need to Know

General

ChatClaw is a voice-powered GenUI assistant ‚Äî an AI app that generates adaptive user interfaces based on your voice input. Instead of navigating menus and buttons, you speak naturally and the right interface appears instantly. It's built on OpenClaw and powered by Claude Opus 4.6.

Platform & Technical

Which platforms are supported?

ChatClaw runs on iOS, Android, and Web. Built with Flutter, it delivers a consistent experience across all platforms. An Apple Watch companion app is also available.

What is Generative UI (GenUI)?

GenUI means the user interface is generated by AI in real time rather than pre-built by developers. When you ask ChatClaw a question, the AI creates the most appropriate visual layout ‚Äî cards, tables, lists, charts ‚Äî tailored to your specific request.

What AI model powers ChatClaw?

ChatClaw is powered by Claude Opus 4.6 from Anthropic, accessed through the OpenClaw gateway. This provides advanced reasoning, multilingual understanding, and context-aware responses.

OpenClaw Integration

How does ChatClaw integrate with OpenClaw?

ChatClaw connects to the OpenClaw gateway as a native message app. Your voice requests are routed through OpenClaw to the appropriate AI skills and agents. Setup is simple ‚Äî enter your gateway URL in settings and start talking. See our integration guide for details.

Do I need OpenClaw to use ChatClaw?

ChatClaw works standalone for basic AI conversation. However, connecting to OpenClaw unlocks the full ecosystem ‚Äî specialized agents, advanced skills, device control, and multi-step workflows.

What is OpenClaw?

OpenClaw is an open AI agent platform that provides a unified gateway to AI skills, tools, and services. ChatClaw is the recommended message app for interacting with the OpenClaw ecosystem.

Privacy & Security

How is my data handled?

ChatClaw uses Firebase for secure authentication and data storage. Voice data is processed for the current session and not stored permanently unless you explicitly save a conversation. We follow industry-standard encryption for data in transit and at rest.

Is my voice data recorded?

Voice input is processed in real time to generate responses. Raw audio is not permanently stored on our servers. Transcriptions may be retained for session continuity based on your privacy settings.

Can I delete my data?

Yes. You can delete your conversation history, account data, and all associated information from within the app settings. Account deletion is permanent and irreversible.
